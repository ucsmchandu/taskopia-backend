# Community Guidelines

## Overview

Taskopia is a community-driven platform built on trust, respect, and responsible behavior.

These Community Guidelines explain the standards expected from every user. Following these guidelines helps create a safe, reliable, and professional environment where Hosts and Allies can confidently work together.

Every user is expected to act honestly, communicate respectfully, and follow the platform's policies while using Taskopia.

---

# Our Community Values

Taskopia is built around five core values.

## Respect

Treat every user with kindness and professionalism.

Respect different backgrounds, cultures, languages, and opinions.

---

## Honesty

Provide truthful information.

Be honest about:

- Task descriptions
- Skills
- Availability
- Payments
- Reviews
- Applications

---

## Responsibility

Take responsibility for your actions.

Complete the commitments you make and communicate if plans change.

---

## Safety

Help maintain a safe community by reporting suspicious or harmful behavior.

---

## Fairness

Treat other users fairly and avoid exploiting the platform or other members.

---

# Expected Behaviour

Users are expected to:

- Be respectful.
- Communicate professionally.
- Use appropriate language.
- Provide accurate information.
- Complete agreed tasks responsibly.
- Respect other users' privacy.
- Follow platform rules.
- Report genuine concerns.

Positive behavior builds trust across the community.

---

# Respectful Communication

Communication should always remain polite and task-focused.

Users should:

- Be courteous.
- Be patient.
- Explain things clearly.
- Respect different opinions.
- Avoid arguments.

Professional communication improves collaboration.

---

# Honesty in Task Posting

Hosts should create genuine task listings.

Task descriptions should accurately explain:

- The work
- The payment
- The location
- The expected duration
- Required skills

Misleading information damages community trust.

---

# Honest Applications

Allies should only apply for tasks they genuinely intend to complete.

Applications should reflect:

- Actual availability
- Real skills
- Honest interest

Submitting fake applications wastes everyone's time.

---

# Respecting Time

Time is valuable for both Hosts and Allies.

Users should:

- Arrive on time.
- Respond promptly.
- Inform others if delayed.
- Avoid unnecessary cancellations.

Reliable users build stronger reputations.

---

# Protecting Privacy

Respect other users' personal information.

Do not request unnecessary details such as:

- Passwords
- Banking information
- Government IDs
- Sensitive personal documents

Only exchange information necessary for completing the task.

---

# Appropriate Content

Taskopia welcomes constructive and professional communication.

Examples of acceptable content:

- Task discussions
- Questions
- Clarifications
- Feedback
- Reviews
- Suggestions

Content should always relate to legitimate platform usage.

---

# Prohibited Behaviour

The following behaviour is not allowed.

## Harassment

Including:

- Bullying
- Insults
- Intimidation
- Personal attacks

---

## Hate Speech

Content targeting individuals or groups based on protected characteristics is prohibited.

---

## Threats

Threatening or encouraging violence is not allowed.

---

## Spam

Users must not send:

- Repeated unwanted messages
- Advertisements
- Promotional content
- Irrelevant messages

---

## Fraud

Fraudulent behaviour includes:

- Fake task postings
- Fake applications
- Misleading payment promises
- Identity impersonation
- Attempting to deceive users

---

## Illegal Activities

Taskopia cannot be used for illegal work or unlawful activities.

Any task violating applicable laws is prohibited.

---

## Sharing Harmful Content

Users must not share:

- Offensive material
- Harmful files
- Malicious links
- Illegal content

---

# Reviews and Ratings

Reviews should be:

- Honest
- Respectful
- Constructive
- Based on real experiences

Users must not submit:

- Fake reviews
- Personal attacks
- Offensive language
- False accusations

---

# Responsible Use of AI Features

Taskopia's AI tools should be used responsibly.

Users must not use AI to:

- Create misleading task descriptions.
- Generate abusive content.
- Spread misinformation.
- Violate platform rules.

AI is intended to assist users, not deceive others.

---

# Reporting Problems

Users should report behaviour that violates these guidelines.

Examples include:

- Fraud
- Harassment
- Abuse
- Fake tasks
- Spam
- Hate speech
- Unsafe behaviour

Reports help maintain community safety.

---

# Consequences of Violations

Violating Community Guidelines may result in:

- Educational warnings
- Content removal
- Temporary restrictions
- Account suspension
- Permanent account termination

The severity of the action depends on the nature and frequency of the violation.

---

# Building a Positive Reputation

Users who consistently behave responsibly are more likely to:

- Receive better ratings.
- Build trust.
- Get selected more often.
- Develop long-term relationships.
- Contribute positively to the community.

Professional behaviour benefits everyone.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I advertise my business through chat?

No.

Chat should be used only for task-related communication.

---

## Can I post fake tasks as a joke?

No.

Every published task must represent genuine work.

---

## Can I use offensive language?

No.

Respectful communication is expected from every user.

---

## What happens if I repeatedly break the rules?

Repeated violations may result in suspension or permanent removal from Taskopia.

---

## Can I report users who violate these guidelines?

Yes.

Use the Report Problem feature to report inappropriate behaviour.

---

## Does Taskopia investigate reports?

Yes.

Reports are reviewed and investigated when necessary.

---

# Summary

Taskopia's Community Guidelines encourage respectful communication, honesty, responsibility, and safety.

By following these guidelines, Hosts and Allies help create a trustworthy marketplace where people can confidently connect, complete tasks, and build lasting reputations.
