# Ratings and Reviews in Taskopia

## Overview

Ratings and reviews help build trust within the Taskopia community.

After a task has been successfully completed, both the Host and the Ally have the opportunity to rate and review each other based on their experience.

These ratings allow future users to make informed decisions before working together.

A strong reputation is earned through consistent, honest, and professional behavior.

---

# Why Ratings Matter

Taskopia is a community-driven platform where trust plays a significant role.

Ratings help users:

- Build credibility.
- Demonstrate reliability.
- Increase confidence among other users.
- Encourage professional behavior.
- Improve the quality of the marketplace.

A user with positive ratings is generally more likely to receive future opportunities.

---

# Who Can Leave Ratings?

After a task has been marked as completed:

- Hosts can rate and review the Ally.
- Allies can rate and review the Host.

Only users who participated in the completed task can submit ratings.

Users cannot rate someone they have never worked with.

---

# Rating Scale

Taskopia uses a five-star rating system.

Typical interpretation:

⭐ 1 Star

Very poor experience.

⭐ 2 Stars

Below expectations.

⭐ 3 Stars

Average experience.

⭐ 4 Stars

Good experience.

⭐ 5 Stars

Excellent experience.

The rating should accurately reflect the user's actual experience.

---

# Writing Reviews

Along with a star rating, users may write a review.

A good review explains the experience clearly and honestly.

Examples include:

- Communication quality
- Professionalism
- Punctuality
- Work quality
- Cooperation
- Reliability

Helpful reviews assist future users in making better decisions.

---

# What Hosts May Review

Hosts often evaluate:

- Arrival on time
- Quality of work
- Professional behavior
- Communication
- Following instructions
- Reliability
- Overall satisfaction

These reviews help identify dependable Allies.

---

# What Allies May Review

Allies may evaluate:

- Accuracy of the task description
- Communication
- Respectful behavior
- Payment experience
- Organization
- Clear instructions
- Overall professionalism

These reviews help other Allies identify trustworthy Hosts.

---

# Importance of Honest Reviews

Reviews should always be based on actual experiences.

Users should avoid:

- False praise
- Personal attacks
- Misleading statements
- Offensive language
- Fake reviews

Honest reviews improve the overall quality of the platform.

---

# Building a Good Reputation

A strong reputation is earned over time.

Hosts can improve their reputation by:

- Writing clear task descriptions.
- Offering fair payment.
- Responding promptly.
- Treating Allies respectfully.
- Completing tasks professionally.

Allies can improve their reputation by:

- Arriving on time.
- Completing quality work.
- Communicating clearly.
- Being reliable.
- Acting professionally.

---

# Benefits of High Ratings

Users with consistently positive ratings may experience:

- Greater trust.
- More applications from Allies.
- Higher chances of being selected.
- Better community reputation.
- Increased confidence among users.

Good ratings reflect consistent positive behavior.

---

# Fair Reviewing Guidelines

When leaving a review:

- Be honest.
- Be respectful.
- Mention facts.
- Avoid emotional language.
- Focus on the task experience.
- Write constructive feedback.

Reviews should help future users understand what to expect.

---

# Inappropriate Reviews

Reviews should never include:

- Abuse
- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal threats
- False accusations
- Private information
- Discriminatory remarks
- Spam

Reviews violating community guidelines may be removed.

---

# Editing Reviews

Depending on future platform features, users may be allowed to edit or update reviews within a limited period.

Once finalized, reviews become part of the user's reputation history.

---

# Reporting Review Abuse

If a review contains abusive or false content, users can report it through the Report Problem feature.

Taskopia may investigate reports involving:

- Fake reviews
- Harassment
- Offensive language
- Spam
- Personal attacks

Action may be taken if community guidelines have been violated.

---

# Reputation in Taskopia

Reputation is built gradually through completed tasks and positive interactions.

A user's reputation reflects:

- Reliability

Maintaining a strong reputation increases confidence among future Hosts and Allies.

To maintain a positive reputation:

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I rate someone before the task is completed?

No.

Ratings are available only after the task has been completed.

---

## Can anyone leave a review?

No.

Only the Host and Ally involved in the completed task can review each other.

---

## Can I leave only a rating?

Depending on platform functionality, users may leave a star rating, a written review, or both.

---

## Can fake reviews be reported?

Yes.

Users can report reviews that violate Taskopia's policies.

---

## Why are ratings important?

Ratings help build trust and allow users to make informed decisions before working together.

---

## Do ratings affect future opportunities?

Yes.

Users with positive ratings generally build stronger reputations, making other users more confident in working with them.

---

# Summary

Ratings and reviews are an essential part of the Taskopia community.
