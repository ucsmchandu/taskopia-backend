# Safety and Trust

## Overview

Taskopia is committed to building a safe, trustworthy, and respectful marketplace where Hosts and Allies can confidently connect for short-term work.

While Taskopia provides the technology that helps users discover tasks, communicate, and report problems, the actual task is completed offline between the Host and the selected Ally.

For this reason, safety is a shared responsibility between Taskopia and its users.

Taskopia continuously works to improve safety through secure platform features, reporting tools, moderation, and transparent user interactions.

---

# Our Commitment

Taskopia is designed to promote trust throughout every stage of the task lifecycle.

We are committed to:

- Providing a secure platform
- Protecting user privacy
- Encouraging respectful communication
- Reducing fraudulent activity
- Supporting users through reporting tools
- Taking action against policy violations
- Continuously improving platform safety

---

# Building Trust

Trust is one of the most important parts of the Taskopia community.

Taskopia encourages trust through:

- Verified user accounts
- Ratings and reviews
- Transparent task information
- Secure authentication
- In-platform communication
- Community reporting
- Platform moderation

Every completed task contributes to a stronger and more reliable community.

---

# Verified Accounts

Taskopia uses secure account authentication to reduce fake accounts and unauthorized access.

Users should:

- Register using accurate information.
- Keep account details updated.
- Protect login credentials.
- Never share passwords.

A secure account helps protect both the user and the community.

---

# Safe Task Posting

Hosts should create honest and accurate task listings.

Every task should clearly describe:

- What work needs to be completed
- The location
- Expected duration
- Payment
- Required skills
- Important instructions

Misleading or incomplete task information may confuse applicants and reduce trust.

---

# Safe Applications

Allies should carefully read every task before applying.

Before submitting an application, Allies should ensure that:

- They understand the work.
- They are available.
- They have the required skills.
- They can travel to the location.
- They are comfortable with the payment offered.

Applying only to suitable tasks benefits both users.

---

# Secure Communication

Taskopia provides an in-platform chat system.

Chat becomes available only after a Host accepts an Ally.

This helps:

- Reduce spam.
- Protect privacy.
- Prevent harassment.
- Keep communication task-focused.

Users should avoid moving conversations outside the platform unless necessary.

---

# AI Translation

Taskopia includes AI-powered translation to help users communicate across different languages.

Translation improves accessibility and reduces misunderstandings between Hosts and Allies.

---

# Meeting Safely

Because tasks are completed offline, users should take appropriate precautions.

Recommended safety practices include:

- Meet in safe, public places whenever possible.
- Inform a trusted person where you are going.
- Arrive during daylight when appropriate.
- Verify important details before meeting.
- Trust your instincts if something feels unsafe.

Personal safety should always come first.

---

# Protecting Personal Information

Users should avoid sharing unnecessary personal information.

Do not share:

- Passwords
- Banking credentials
- Government identification numbers
- Financial information
- Sensitive personal documents

Only share information necessary to complete the task.

---

# Payment Safety

Hosts and Allies should agree on payment details before the task begins.

Both users should understand:

- Payment amount
- Payment method
- When payment will be made

Clear agreements reduce misunderstandings.

---

- Communicate politely.

- Safety concerns

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Taskopia responsible for offline work?

No.

Taskopia provides the platform that connects users.

Offline interactions remain the responsibility of the participating users.

---

## Can I report abusive users?

Yes.

Users can report misconduct using the Report Problem feature.

---

## Does Taskopia monitor chats?

Taskopia may review conversations when investigating reports or policy violations.

Routine conversations are not manually monitored.

---

## How can I stay safe?

Users should:

- Meet safely.
- Keep communication respectful.
- Protect personal information.
- Report suspicious activity.
- Follow platform guidelines.

---

## What happens if someone breaks the rules?

Taskopia may investigate the incident and take appropriate action, including warnings, suspension, or permanent account removal.
