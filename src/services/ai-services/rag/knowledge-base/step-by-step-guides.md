# Step-by-Step Guides

## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for common actions performed in Taskopia.

Whether you are a new user or an existing member, these guides explain how to use the platform effectively.

---

# How to Create a Host Account

A Host account allows you to post tasks and hire Allies.

## Steps

1. Open the Taskopia website.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Choose **Host** as your account type.
4. Enter your full name.
5. Enter your email address.
6. Create a secure password.
7. Complete the registration process.
8. Log in using your new account.

You are now ready to post tasks.

---

# How to Create an Ally Account

An Ally account allows you to browse and apply for tasks.

## Steps

1. Open Taskopia.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Select **Ally** as your account type.
4. Enter your personal information.
5. Create a password.
6. Complete registration.
7. Log in.

You can now browse available tasks.

---

# How to Log In

## Steps

1. Open Taskopia.
2. Click **Login**.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Enter your password.
5. Click **Login**.

You will be redirected to your dashboard.

---

# How to Reset Your Password

## Steps

1. Click **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Submit the request.
4. Follow the password reset instructions.
5. Create a new password.
6. Log in using the new password.

---

# How to Create a Task

Only Hosts can create tasks.

## Steps

1. Log in as a Host.
2. Open the Host Dashboard.
3. Click **Create Task**.
4. Enter the task title.
5. Enter the task description.
6. Select a category.
7. Choose the task location.
8. Enter the payment amount.
9. Select the date and time.
10. Add any additional instructions.
11. Review the information.
12. Click **Publish Task**.

Your task is now visible to Allies.

---

# How to Use AI to Generate a Task Description

Taskopia provides AI assistance while creating tasks.

## Steps

1. Begin creating a task.
2. Fill in the basic task information.
3. Click **Generate Description**.
4. Wait for the AI to generate a description.
5. Review the generated text.
6. Edit it if necessary.
7. Save the task.

---

# How to Browse Tasks

Only Allies can browse available tasks.

## Steps

1. Log in as an Ally.
2. Open the task marketplace.
3. Browse available tasks.
4. Use filters if necessary.
5. Open any task to read its details.

---

# How to Search for Tasks

## Steps

1. Open the task marketplace.
2. Use the search bar.
3. Enter keywords.
4. Apply filters such as:
   - Category
   - Location
   - Budget
   - Date
5. Review the matching tasks.

---

# How to Apply for a Task

## Steps

1. Open the task details page.
2. Read the entire description.
3. Confirm that you are available.
4. Click **Apply**.
5. Wait for the Host to review your application.

---

# What Happens After Applying?

After applying:

1. Your application is submitted.
2. The Host reviews applicants.
3. One Ally is selected.
4. If accepted, you receive a notification.
5. Chat becomes available.

---

# How to Review Applications

Only Hosts can review applications.

## Steps

1. Open the Host Dashboard.
2. Select your task.
3. Open the Applications section.
4. Review each applicant.
5. Compare profiles.
6. Choose one Ally.

---

# How to Accept an Ally

## Steps

1. Open your task.
2. Review applications.
3. Select the preferred Ally.
4. Click **Accept**.

The Ally is notified immediately.

Chat is now enabled.

---

# How to Start a Conversation

After acceptance:

1. Open the Chat section.
2. Select the accepted task.
3. Send your first message.

Communication should remain focused on the task.

---

# How to Complete a Task

## For Allies

1. Meet the Host.
2. Complete the agreed work.
3. Inform the Host when finished.

## For Hosts

1. Verify the work.
2. Confirm completion.
3. Mark the task as completed.

---

# How to Leave a Rating

After completion:

1. Open the completed task.
2. Select **Leave Review**.
3. Choose a star rating.
4. Write an optional review.
5. Submit.

---

# How to Report a Problem

## Steps

1. Open the **Report Problem** page.
2. Select the issue type.
3. Enter the required details.
4. Describe the incident.
5. Submit the report.

Taskopia will review the report.

---

# How to Edit Your Profile

## Steps

1. Open your profile.
2. Click **Edit Profile**.
3. Update your information.
4. Save your changes.

---

# How to Change Your Password

## Steps

1. Open Account Settings.
2. Select **Change Password**.
3. Enter your current password.
4. Enter a new password.
5. Confirm the new password.
6. Save the changes.

---

# How to Log Out

## Steps

1. Open the account menu.
2. Click **Logout**.

Your session ends immediately.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I edit a task after publishing it?

Yes, provided the task has not been completed.

---

## Can I apply to multiple tasks?

Yes.

---

## Can I message the Host before acceptance?

No.

Chat becomes available only after the Host accepts your application.

---

## What if my application is rejected?

You can continue applying to other available tasks.

---

## Can I cancel a task?

Yes.

Hosts can cancel tasks according to platform rules.

---

## Can I edit my profile anytime?

Yes.

Users can update their profile whenever necessary.

---

# Summary

Taskopia is designed to keep every workflow simple and intuitive.

Whether creating an account, posting a task, applying for work, communicating through chat, or completing a task, users can follow these step-by-step guides to use the platform efficiently and confidently.
