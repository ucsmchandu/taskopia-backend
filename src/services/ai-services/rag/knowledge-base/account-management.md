# Account Management

## Overview

An account is required to access Taskopia's features.

Creating an account allows users to participate in the Taskopia community as either a Host or an Ally.

Taskopia uses secure authentication to protect user accounts and provide personalized experiences.

Each account is associated with a specific role and contains the user's profile, tasks, applications, ratings, reviews, and activity history.

---

# Account Types

Taskopia currently supports two account types.

## Host Account

A Host account is designed for users who need temporary help.

Hosts can:

- Create tasks
- Manage tasks
- Review applications
- Accept an Ally
- Chat with accepted Allies
- Complete tasks
- Leave ratings and reviews

Examples of Hosts include:

- Homeowners
- Shop owners
- Small businesses
- Event organizers
- Individuals

---

## Ally Account

An Ally account is designed for users who want to complete tasks and earn income.

Allies can:

- Browse tasks
- Search tasks
- Apply for work
- Chat with accepted Hosts
- Complete assigned tasks
- Receive ratings and reviews

Examples include:

- Students
- Freelancers
- Skilled workers
- Part-time workers
- Local residents

---

# Separate Accounts

Taskopia requires separate accounts for Hosts and Allies.

A single account cannot perform both roles.

This separation helps:

- Keep workflows simple.
- Prevent misuse.
- Improve platform organization.
- Maintain accurate task management.

If a user wants to act as both a Host and an Ally, they should create separate accounts for each role.

---

# Creating an Account

Creating a Taskopia account is free.

To register:

1. Choose your account type.
2. Enter your name.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Create a secure password.
5. Complete registration.
6. Log in to your new account.

Some platform versions may require email verification before the account becomes active.

---

# Logging In

Registered users can log in using their credentials.

Users typically provide:

- Email address
- Password

Successful authentication grants access to the user's dashboard and account features.

---

# Logging Out

Users can securely log out of their account at any time.

Logging out is recommended when using shared or public devices.

---

# Forgot Password

If a user forgets their password, they can reset it.

The password reset process generally involves:

1. Selecting "Forgot Password."
2. Entering the registered email.
3. Receiving password reset instructions.
4. Creating a new password.
5. Logging in with the updated password.

# Profile Picture

Users may upload a profile picture.

A profile picture helps:

- Improve recognition

Users should upload appropriate images that follow platform guidelines.

---

# Email Address

The registered email is used for:

- Login
- Password recovery
- Platform notifications
- Account-related communication

Users should ensure that their email address remains active and accessible.

---

# Password Security

Users should create strong passwords.

A secure password should:

- Be difficult to guess.
- Contain uppercase letters.
- Contain lowercase letters.
- Include numbers.
- Include special characters.

Passwords should never be shared with anyone.

---

# Account Security

Users should protect their accounts by:

- Keeping passwords private.
- Logging out from shared devices.
- Avoiding suspicious links.
- Reporting unauthorized access.
- Updating passwords when necessary.

Maintaining account security protects both the user and the community.

- Reviews

- Reports submitted

Account holders may receive notifications about important platform events.

# Account Restrictions

Accounts may be temporarily or permanently restricted if users violate platform policies.

Examples include:

- Fraud
- Harassment
- Spam
- Fake tasks
- Fake applications
- Offensive behaviour
- Illegal activities

Restrictions help maintain a safe community.

---

# Account Suspension

Taskopia may suspend accounts while investigating serious violations.

During suspension, users may temporarily lose access to platform features.

---

# Account Termination

Accounts may be permanently removed if users repeatedly violate platform policies or engage in serious misconduct.

Permanent termination may result in the loss of access to platform services.

---

# Deleting an Account

Users may request account deletion if they no longer wish to use Taskopia.

Before deleting an account, users should:

- Complete active tasks.
- Resolve pending issues.
- Save important information if necessary.

Deleted accounts may lose access to platform history, ratings, and associated data according to Taskopia's policies.

---

To maintain a healthy account:

- Protect your password.
- Communicate respectfully.
- Follow platform rules.
- Report suspicious behaviour.
