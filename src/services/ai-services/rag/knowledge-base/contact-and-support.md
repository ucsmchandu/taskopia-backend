# Contact and Support

## Overview

Taskopia provides a Contact and Support system to help users resolve platform-related questions, report issues, provide feedback, and request assistance.

If users cannot find the answer in the Help Center or through the AI Help Assistant, they can contact the Taskopia support team directly.

The support team reviews every request and responds as quickly as possible.

---

# When to Contact Support

Users should contact Taskopia when they need assistance with platform-related issues.

Common reasons include:

- Account problems
- Login issues
- Password recovery
- Task-related questions
- Application issues
- Payment-related questions
- Safety concerns
- Technical problems
- Platform bugs
- Feature requests
- General feedback

---

# Contact Form

Taskopia provides a Contact Us form where users can submit their requests.

The form collects the following information:

## Name

Users should enter their full name.

This helps the support team identify the request.

---

## Email Address

Users should provide a valid email address.

The support team uses this email to send responses or request additional information.

---

## Subject

Users should choose the subject that best matches their issue.

Available subjects include:

- Account related issue
- Task or application issue
- Payment related question
- Safety or trust concern
- General feedback

Selecting the correct subject helps route the request to the appropriate support process.

---

## Message

Users should clearly describe their issue or question.

A good support message includes:

- What happened
- When it happened
- Which task was involved (if applicable)
- Any error messages
- Steps already taken
- Expected outcome

Providing detailed information helps the support team resolve the issue more efficiently.

---

# Response Time

Taskopia aims to respond to support requests within:

**24 to 48 hours**

Response times may be longer during:

- Weekends
- Public holidays
- High support volume

Urgent issues are reviewed as quickly as possible.

---

# Types of Issues We Can Help With

Support can assist with:

## Account Issues

Examples:

- Unable to log in
- Password reset
- Profile updates
- Account access problems

---

## Task Issues

Examples:

- Problems creating tasks
- Editing tasks
- Cancelling tasks
- Application questions
- Task status questions

---

## Payment Questions

Examples:

- Payment process clarification
- Payment-related guidance
- Budget questions

Taskopia may provide guidance but payment agreements remain between the Host and the Ally.

---

## Safety Concerns

Users should contact support regarding:

- Suspicious behaviour
- Fake accounts
- Harassment
- Abuse
- Fraud
- Privacy concerns

Serious safety issues should also be reported using the Report Problem feature.

---

## Technical Problems

Support can investigate technical issues such as:

- Pages not loading
- Login failures
- Broken features
- Unexpected errors
- Performance issues

Providing screenshots or detailed descriptions may help diagnose the problem.

---

## Feedback and Suggestions

Taskopia welcomes feedback from the community.

Users can submit:

- Feature requests
- Improvement suggestions
- User experience feedback
- General comments

Community feedback helps improve future versions of the platform.

---

# Tips for Contacting Support

Before submitting a request:

- Describe the issue clearly.
- Include relevant task information if applicable.
- Mention any error messages.
- Explain what you expected to happen.
- Provide accurate contact information.

Clear messages help reduce response time.

---

# What Support Cannot Help With

Support may not be able to assist with:

- Personal disputes unrelated to the platform
- Illegal activities
- Emergency situations
- Requests that violate Taskopia policies

Taskopia also cannot make decisions on behalf of users regarding private agreements made outside the platform.

---

# Emergency Situations

The Contact Us form is **not** intended for emergencies.

If a user is in immediate danger or requires emergency assistance, they should contact the appropriate local emergency services.

---

# Respectful Communication

Users should communicate respectfully when contacting support.

Support requests should not contain:

- Abuse
- Threats
- Hate speech
- Offensive language
- Spam
- Misleading information

Abusive or fraudulent messages may not receive a response and may result in account action.

---

# Other Support Resources

Before contacting support, users may also find answers through:

- AI Help Assistant
- Help Center
- Frequently Asked Questions
- Platform documentation

These resources often provide immediate answers to common questions.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I contact Taskopia?

Use the Contact Us form available on the platform.

---

## What information should I include?

Include your name, email address, subject, and a detailed explanation of the issue.

---

## How long does it take to receive a response?

Most requests receive a response within 24 to 48 hours.

---

## Can I report a safety issue through Contact Us?

Yes, but the Report Problem feature is recommended for reporting abuse, harassment, fraud, or other policy violations.

---

## Can I submit feedback?

Yes.

Taskopia encourages users to share ideas, suggestions, and feedback to help improve the platform.

---

## Can support recover my password?

Support can guide users through the password recovery process if needed.

---

# Summary

Taskopia's Contact and Support system provides users with a direct way to receive assistance, report problems, ask questions, and share feedback.

By providing accurate information and clearly describing the issue, users help the support team resolve requests efficiently while contributing to a better experience for the entire Taskopia community.
