# Common User Questions

## General Questions

### Q: What is Taskopia?

Taskopia is a community-based marketplace that connects people who need temporary help (Hosts) with people who are willing to complete short-term tasks (Allies). It focuses on local, flexible, task-based work rather than permanent employment.

---

### Q: Is Taskopia free?

Yes.

Creating an account and using the platform is free.

---

### Q: Who can use Taskopia?

Anyone who wants to post short-term work or complete short-term work can use Taskopia.

---

### Q: What is the difference between a Host and an Ally?

A Host creates and manages tasks.

An Ally applies for and completes tasks.

---

### Q: Can I be both a Host and an Ally?

No.

Taskopia currently requires separate accounts for each role.

---

## Account Questions

### Q: How do I create an account?

Choose your account type, enter your information, create a password, and complete registration.

---

### Q: I forgot my password.

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page and follow the password reset instructions.

---

### Q: Can I change my profile information?

Yes.

Users can update their profile at any time.

---

## Task Questions

### Q: How do I post a task?

Log in as a Host, open the task creation page, enter task details, and publish the task.

---

### Q: Can I edit my task?

Yes.

Tasks can be edited before they are completed.

---

### Q: Can I delete my task?

Yes.

Hosts may remove tasks according to platform rules.

---

### Q: How many Allies can apply?

There is no fixed limit on applications.

Multiple Allies may apply.

---

### Q: How many Allies can I accept?

Only one Ally can be accepted for each task.

---

### Q: Can I cancel my task?

Yes.

Hosts can cancel tasks before completion.

---

## Application Questions

### Q: How do I apply for a task?

Open the task details page and submit your application.

---

### Q: Can I apply for multiple tasks?

Yes.

Allies may apply for multiple tasks.

---

### Q: Does applying guarantee selection?

No.

The Host chooses the Ally.

---

### Q: What happens after I apply?

The Host reviews applications and decides whether to accept one.

---

### Q: How do I know if I am selected?

You'll receive a notification when the Host accepts your application.

---

## Chat Questions

### Q: When can I chat?

Only after the Host accepts your application.

---

### Q: Why can't I message the Host?

To reduce spam and protect user privacy.

---

### Q: Does Taskopia support multiple languages?

Yes.

AI Translation helps users communicate in different languages.

---

## Payment Questions

### Q: Who decides the payment?

The Host sets the payment while creating the task.

---

### Q: Does Taskopia pay me?

No.

Payment is handled according to the agreement between the Host and the Ally.

---

### Q: Can I negotiate payment?

Users may discuss task-related details after acceptance.

---

## Ratings

### Q: Can I rate another user?

Yes.

Ratings become available after task completion.

---

### Q: Can anyone review me?

Only users who completed a task with you.

---

## Safety

### Q: Is Taskopia safe?

Taskopia provides secure authentication, ratings, reporting tools, moderation, and in-app communication to improve safety.

---

### Q: How do I report someone?

Use the Report Problem feature.

---

### Q: What should I do if someone abuses me?

Report the user immediately through the platform.

---

- AI Translation

No.

### Q: Can AI translate messages?

Yes.

Taskopia supports AI-powered translation.

Yes.

Taskopia works on mobile devices and desktop browsers.

### Q: Does Taskopia keep my data private?
